HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTO
=====================
1. Name your photo file exactly:  profile.jpg
2. Place it in this folder:       /public/profile.jpg
3. Recommended size:              700×900 px (portrait), face/shoulders visible at the top

The website will automatically display it in the hero section.
The fallback "KS" initials will show until you add the file.

HOW TO ADD YOUR CV PDF
======================
1. Export your CV as a PDF
2. Name it exactly:               kaylee-schneider-cv.pdf
3. Place it in this folder:       /public/kaylee-schneider-cv.pdf

Both the "Download CV" buttons will then work automatically.
